# guild_master_cell

- `guild_master_cell/` is a reusable workflow-chain party for skill authoring, post-development review, and strategic gap review.
- The party enters through `author_skill_package`, may hand off to `post_development_review_gate_v0`, and may route strategic gaps to `ouroboros_strategic_review_harness_v0`.
- Workflow step order and optimized execution profiles remain under `.workflow/**`.
- Slot files in this folder are compatibility/fallback metadata only; they are not the source of optimized model, effort, species, class, or unit choices.
