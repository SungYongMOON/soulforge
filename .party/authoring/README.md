# .party/authoring

`authoring/` holds party drafts before they are promoted into the active party
catalog.

Draft parties may describe a cadence chain, local automation relationship, and
future workflow binding. They are not active `.party/index.yaml` entries, do
not switch default routes, and do not change Codex app automation state.
