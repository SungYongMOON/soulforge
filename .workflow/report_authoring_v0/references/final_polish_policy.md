# Final-polish policy

`final_polish` may improve organization, readability and natural practitioner tone.
Write for the actual reader, keep necessary technical terms and qualifiers, and use
active or passive voice according to actor relevance and domain convention.
It may convert unprotected prose into functional headings, bullets, or tables and
derive a reader summary after body verification. A declared protected surface stays
at its original anchor in v0. It is therefore a report-structuring pass with a
meaning freeze, not a cosmetic synonym filter.

There is no AI-detector target, detector-evasion objective, forbidden-word list,
fluency score or document-level similarity gate. Do not delete limitations,
uncertainty, evidence links or technical distinctions merely to shorten the text.

Protected meaning has zero default tolerance for omission or mutation. Sentence
splits and voice changes are allowed only where every declared protected surface,
anchor, association, role, scope, condition, modality, and source trace stays
unchanged. v0 does not implement an authorized-change record, so citation
renumbering, unit conversion, or movement of protected content is rejected even
when a human might judge the proposed change equivalent. Any detected
protected-field delta blocks the run and routes the artifact back to
technical-content review.

Use function rules, not vocabulary rules:

- Give each sentence one primary job: evidence, interpretation, conclusion,
  action request, or limit.
- Put the decision-relevant result early while keeping its condition and qualifier
  attached.
- State observations directly and calibrate interpretation to a named basis.
- Name the actor when responsibility, provenance, or assurance matters; do not
  force active voice when the actor is unknown or irrelevant.
- Keep value, unit, uncertainty/tolerance, coverage basis, comparator, and
  condition together.
- Repeat an exact technical noun when a pronoun could change entity binding.
- Preserve negation and explicit unknowns. Do not turn sequence into causality or
  absence of evidence into evidence of absence.
- State failures plainly and make recommendations actionable only with supplied
  actor, action, trigger/date, and close condition.
- Use complete sentences for reasoning. Compact noun endings are optional for
  headings, cards, and checklist bullets.

Forbidden transformations are meaning-based: adding unsupported content; removing
conditions, limitations, attribution, citations, or uncertainty; changing number,
unit, comparator, modality, role, scope, causality, table association, or verdict;
and making summary/body/table/conclusion/action surfaces disagree. A suspected
semantic improvement is an authorized change request, not a silent polish edit.

The final reader projection contains report prose and permitted document metadata
only. Exclude stage names, prompts, gap registers, claim IDs, manifests, validator
output, receipts, reviewer notes, runtime paths, and template placeholders by
field role and provenance. Do not reject legitimate report prose merely because it
contains the same vocabulary as an internal artifact.
